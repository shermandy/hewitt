A Brief Mission Statement

The mission of the Hewitt Presbyterian Church is to affirm our oneness in Jesus Christ by reaching out to all people, to share our spiritual gifts, and welcome them in celebrating the joys, freedoms, and responsibilities that come with our faith in God.
