Rev. William Parker

Greetings in the name of our Lord, Jesus Christ! I am excited to be a part of the ongoing ministry of the Hewitt Presbyterian Church, and can't wait to meet and get to know each one of you. My wife Sara, and our children Matthew and Kara, are blessed to have this opportunity to journey with you in the building up of the kingdom of God in the Rices Landing area. We are home grown Greene Countians and love this corner of western Pennsylvania. I have been in ministry for 34 years and Sara has been a teacher for 15 years. Our family enjoys camping, sports, motorcycling, and water parks. We pray God's blessing on our future together.
